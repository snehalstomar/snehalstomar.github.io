Snehal Singh Tomar

Visual and Embodied AI Group, TCS Research | Resume

snehal@smail.iitm.ac.in

I am a pre-doctoral fellow with the Visual and Embodied AI group at TCS Research, mentored by Dr. Lokender Tiwari and Dr. Brojeshwar Bhowmick. I recently graduated from the Indian Institute of Technology Madras with an MS (by Research) in Electrical Engineering, having received the Institute Research Award for excellence in research. I was a part of the Image Processing and Computer Vision (IPCV) Lab, advised by Prof. A.N. Rajagopalan. My research interests are in Deep Generative Visual Models and Self-Supervised Deep Learning methods for Computer Vision applications. I also enjoy working on problems in Computational Photography and 3D Computer Vision. My graduate education was supported by the Ministry of Education, Govt. of India through a Half Time Research Assistantship (HTRA).

Previously, as an undergrad, I did several research internships with Prof. Shubhendu Bhasin at IIT Delhi, working in the domain of Control Systems and Robotics. I was also a part of my university’s AI Robotics Team, where we worked on building an autonomous car.
